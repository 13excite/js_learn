Several facts:

  • 1991 - Torvalds first encountered the GNU Project.
  • March 14, 1994 - The first prototypes of Linux were publicly released.
  • 1999 - Red Hat and VA Linux, both leading developers of Linux-based software, presented Torvalds with stock options in gratitude for his creation.
  • 2000 - received a doctorate from the University of Helsinki.
